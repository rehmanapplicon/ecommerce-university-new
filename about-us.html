Who We Are

ECOMMERCE HIGHER EDUCATION COMMISION

The EHEC is a network of 195 countries and territories that have agreed to follow a common set of principles and practices in higher education associated with ecommerce, artificial intelligence, and information technology, etc. with the aim of promoting mobility, cooperation, and mutual recognition of qualifications. The EHEC is supported by a netwrok of integrated agreements between multi sectorial organizations across the world that aim to create an exclusive Higher Education Area for Ecommerce by making academic degree standards and quality assurance standards more comparable and compatible throughout the world. EHEC has developed a regulatory framework to introduce the above as a global franchse of education and exmaination system with simply one objective through its advanced AI Modular Education Enchancement Learniing: Every Human Being is an Idea!

What We Do

“We are aimed at creating ten million high net worth individuals”

This statement represents our commitment to this cause, and completely reflects the potential of technology in poverty alleviation. Hence, the use of artificial intelligence interfaces and technologies come as expertise ensemble intertwined with easy community integration. The micro and macrocosm components of economies can no longer be based around traditional models or else the world would not have seen the likes of ordinary individuals taking wealth to new heights in the modern times. Hence, as we expanded the scope of our Ecommerce University beyond traditional roles in the twentieth century to more dynamic, vibrant business facilitators and enablers, our strategy focused the changes it could bring at a micro level through adopting Ecommerce and technologies to reflect its benefits on the lives of common individuals.
