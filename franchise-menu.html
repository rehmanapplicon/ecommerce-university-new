Choosing a Region

You are not buying a fast food or a laundry franchise with regional access only, but a prestigious brand with global reach right from the get-go. You are about to lead a developing, thriving international business community with millions of users, and hence the business dynamics require a certain level of acumen and what the business model offers.

Under each country and region, we have provided an updated country summary with its economic overview using CIA’s handbook as a reference to provide insight into a country’s business environment. However, we would also like to advise to also please use your own sources and or either take help from https://data.worldbank.org/country in more detail.

Regardless of the territory’s name by investing in your company, you will also become stockholders of the parent company arranged as a public ltd. company in the US and which may require legal customization according to the local territorial laws.
However, the following come regardless of any constraining factors:

  • A robust training and support mechanism
  • Clear financial forecasts, predictions and targets
  • An absolute strong and visible brand identity
  • A wide range of products and services incorporated within the business model

The geographical territory you choose will be considered the basic unit of the overall business planning and control with an outreach designed around an expected billion plus B2B, B2C, B2G, etc customers intertwined with one another on one platform.
Following are the important aspects and characteristics while considering your operational city or area which may be local, provincial, national or international.

  • Knowledge of present and potential customers
  • Specific classes of customers
  • Business potential of your geography
